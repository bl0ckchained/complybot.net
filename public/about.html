Our Mission: Accessibility for All

Why we built ComplyBot — and how we’re making the web better, one page at a time.

Who We Are

ComplyBot.net is a mission-driven startup based in Michigan, built to help small business owners protect their websites and serve every visitor equally — including those with disabilities.

After learning firsthand how expensive and confusing accessibility compliance can be, we set out to build a tool that makes it easy, affordable, and fast to stay on the right side of the law — and do the right thing.

Why We Care

Over 1 billion people around the world live with a disability. Many rely on assistive technologies like screen readers, keyboard navigation, or captions to interact with the web. Sadly, most websites still block or ignore them.

We believe web access is a civil right. ComplyBot helps eliminate the barriers — and helps businesses avoid costly lawsuits, bad press, and lost customers.
