Help Center

Common questions and answers about ComplyBot accessibility tools and services.

+ What does ComplyBot scan for?
We scan for WCAG 2.1 Level AA issues, including alt text, contrast errors, keyboard access, and ARIA violations.
+ What happens after I run a free scan?
You'll receive a summary of violations. You can then choose to purchase a full audit or remediation services.
+ Is ComplyBot ADA-compliant?
ComplyBot helps you meet ADA and WCAG guidelines, but legal compliance depends on continuous accessibility efforts.
+ Do I need technical skills to use ComplyBot?
Nope! Everything is built for non-tech users. We explain fixes in plain English and offer hands-on help if needed.
